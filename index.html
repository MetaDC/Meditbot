Who We Are

At MediBot, we combine advanced robotics with pharmacist expertise to simplify prescription dispensing for community pharmacies. Our system automates the process from prescription upload to next-day collection ensuring precision, compliance, and efficiency.

We help pharmacies reduce operational workload, eliminate dispensing errors, and enhance profitability by allowing staff to dedicate more time to consultations, NHS services, and patient engagement.
